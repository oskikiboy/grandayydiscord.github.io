Rules.

Breaking one of the rules can result to a warn or a ban depending on how many the person has and what the rule is.
Also, upon joining, if you are warned within the first few hours, and you leave, that will count as a raid.
  • No racial intolerance.
    It doesn't matter what type of racial intolerance you are trying to type, it is still not permitted at all on this server.
    This includes: example (duster do this) and yep.
  • Extreme hate speech.
    Of course opinions are allowed as long as they do not contain extreme hate speech. Breaking this rule will result in an instant ban.
  • Respect the server members.
    Don't be mean to them. They are here for the same reason you are.
    This does include DM spam, bullying/harassing and by deleting their messages.
    If you are a net/villain and you are going to ban someone, please talk about it in #staff.
  • Don't leak anyone's personal info.
    It's wrong and not allowed, we’ll ban you instantly. This includes sharing n00ts (personal info), and DM’s, without their express permission.
  • No NSFW.
    I know it's a bit hard without it but we don't allow it on our server. If you post a NSFW pic/vid/link/air, you will be banned instantly.
  • Don't impersonate anyone.
    It's annoying ok?
    This includes grandayy, bots, me, other server members, and profile pictures.
    Don't ping yourself as grandayy then changing your name afterwards, it's cooooonfusing for us staff members.
    And don’t pull shit with setting your name to grandayyy and saying it’s not the same.
  • Listen to the staff team.
    You must no matter what. If they say to stop it and get some help, you stop it and get some help. It's easy as that.
  • Don't ping anyone without a reason or repeatly.
    You can't just ping people and think it's ok. There has to be a reason.
    Especially if grandayy or the person you mentioning stated to not to be pinged, just don't ping them.
  • Please don't speak in another language.
    This is an English based server, so English is the primary language we all communicate in.
    Refrain from speaking in other languages.
    We can't understand it.
  • Don't be annoying.
    There is no need for a person to spam or annoying us when we are doing something, so please stop ruining the fun for all of us.
    Or we will do it back.
  • Don't try to frame different members about a raid.
    Just give away the info or don't warn us.
    Simple as that.
  • No alts.
    If you have a alt invited to the server, we will ban the alt and warn the main account.
  • No spam of ANY kind.
    It's annoying.
    (This includes reactions and text walls, and applies to Direct/Private Messages with server members)
  • Don't beg for a role.
    This is one of the things we don't really need to read.
    If we think you deserve a role we will give you it. If you reached a level which gets you a role, normally if you wait a few minutes, mee6 will give it to you, if that does not happen for some reason, ping an online villain, and they’ll give you the role.
  • Use channels the way they are intended.
    There’s a channel description for all channels at the top.
  • Don’t play earrape in any voice chat.
    It’s only allowed if everyone in the music-channel agrees to it and must be turned off when they have all had enough.
    So if you’re with others in a channel, don’t play earrape just for fun.
  • Only advertise in #pics-advertise-vids.
    Don’t post the same link/picture too often. This will count as spam.
  • Keep most bot commands in #botcommands.
    Like t!profile, p.invert, !rank, and music commands.
    But small commands like t!reverse and p.say is fine, as it's basically just a different way of chatting.
    This includes the message replies with xayah.
    However, don’t use the bot too much in #robbies-hideout (song lyrics and gifs) for that will be warnnale for spam.
    Use #botcommands for that instead
    The use of voting commands is prohibited to the #botcommands channel or a specific channel created for voting (EX: for events).
  • Post pics in #pics-advertise-vids.
    Only post pictures in #robbies-hideout if it's relevant to what is being talked about.
    Pirates+ can post pics there.
    If the image isn't relevant to the conversation, please post it in #pics-advertise-vids.
